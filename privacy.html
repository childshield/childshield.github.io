Privacy

Factors
The privacy protection of an IoToy can be determined by observing the following factors:
  • P1: Privacy Policy Availability
  • P2: Privacy Policy Accessibility
  • P3: Privacy Policy Simplification
  • P4: Parental Consent
  • P5: Personal Information (PI) Collection
  • P6: Personal Information (PI) Control
  • P7: Third Party Sharing
  • P8: Ads/Marketing Campaigns
  • P9: Support/Helpline Availability
