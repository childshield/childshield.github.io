Penpal Whizz

JLB Educational Technology is a Singapore based company that produces a learning pen called PenPal Whizz for children aged 2 and above. The pen is compatible with many international titles and publishers. When tapped on a compatible book, it can read the text. It can record children's voices as well as play MP3 files.

From Dec 2020 to Feb 2021, JLB participated in a rating and labelling exercise for their Penpal Whizz product. The exercise comprised of the following steps:

  1. Communication of research background and rating methodology
  2. Preliminary study and analysis
  3. Manufacturer response on preliminary study and analysis
  4. Initial rating and recommendations
  5. Implementation of recommendations
  6. Recalculation of rating after completion of quick fixes
  7. Process feedback
The next section presents the primary and secondary labels for Penpal Whizz at the end of the exercise.

S1 Personal Information (PI) Storage Security ✓ Storage is protected for confidentiality, integrity and availability of personal information.

S3 App Privileges NA No companion desktop or mobile app is needed for the functioning of the pen.
S4 Code Security ☑ Some secure coding practices are followed.
S5 Device Security ❌ The device has no networking capability. However the downloaded firmware and audio files are not authenticated prior to execution.
S6 Maintenance ✓ Patches/updates/bug fixes are rolled out from time to time. The latest firmware can be downloaded from manufacturer website.
